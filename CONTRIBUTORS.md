## How to Sign

When you open a pull request, the CLA Assistant bot will post a comment asking you to
sign. Reply to that comment with:

> I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA

Your agreement is recorded automatically in `signatures/cla.json`. You only need to
sign once; future pull requests will be recognized automatically.
